YOUTH

Sundays - 5:30-7:30 P.M.
203 E Locust St, DeKalb, IL 60115

Our middle school and high school students meet as a small group once a week at our church offices. During these times, students will play some fun games, engage in age appropriate discussions about who Jesus is, and learn how to apply the Bible to their lives.

During weekend services, our students participate in the adult worship service and serve alongside the rest of the church.

We place a high standard of character on all of the leaders who serve to help lead our youth. They are people who are committed to providing mature examples of faith in Jesus as well as a safe atmosphere where our students can experience Jesus. All of the volunteers that work with our students are expected to meet the same biblical standards that are required of our adult small group leaders.
